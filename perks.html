Perks

Perks are marks of achievement and signals of arcs in one‚Äôs life journey. Your Class grants you a Perk every odd Level (3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, etc.), as well as through a 1st Level Feature. A Referee may grant additional Perks as a mark of great achievement during the Story.

Prerequisites

Indicated below the Perk‚Äôs name are any Prerequisites required to take it. The exception is when a specific Perk is granted through a Class Feature. In this case, you do not need to meet the Prerequisite to benefit from it.

You can‚Äôt take Perks more than once unless its description says otherwise.

  • Stat: The Stat and Score required to take it. A plus (+) indicates it must be that score or higher.
  • Trained: The Skill or Weapon you must be Trained with to take the Perk.
  • Other: Requires knowledge of a Spell or some other prerequisite that might be Story-based.

Checks you make to Grapple or Shove Targets that are smaller than you are Favored, and you can use them as a greatclub with the Brawl property that deals its damage to both the Target and itself on a hit. Your attacks with it count as maintaining the grapple.

You reduce any fall damage you take by half and, while you are Prone, you can stand up using only 5 feet of Speed.
