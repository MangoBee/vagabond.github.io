Ragged Hollow
Vagabond
Quick Rules
Vagabond is a tabletop roleplaying game built off the foundations of DnD B/X, Pathfinder, Dragonbane, Errant, Knave, Cairn, Into the Odd, Shadowdark, Shadow of the Demon Lord, and other OSR-style games.

Skills & Passives

The numbers that determine the results of rolls are known as Stats. There are six Stats: Might, Dexterity, Awareness, Reason, Presence, and Luck. Each Stat is associated with various Passives, Saves, Skills, and Weapons.

Might

Might is used for Max HP, Inventory Slots, Endure Saves, and both Brawl and Melee Checks.

Max HP (Passive)

Your Maximum Hit Points (Max HP) are equal to your Might × your Level. You regain lost HP when you Rest, as well as by any additional means in-game.

Inventory Slots (Passive)

Your total number of Inventory Slots is equal to (8 + Might).

You cannot carry any items that would exceed your maximum number of Slots. Coins don't count as Items for the purposes of Item Slots. Loot to your heart's content.

A set of clothing doesn't count against your Slots when worn (Armor isn't considered clothing).

The number of Slots an Item occupies follows these general guidelines:

Slots Description
0 1 to 10 of this only occupies 1 Slot.
1 It can be carried in one hand.
2 It requires two hands to carry.
3 It requires two hands to carry, and is heavy.

Melee (Skill)

Used when attacking with Melee Weapons.

Brawl (Skill)

Used for exerting raw physical force. This includes Grapples, Shoves, and attacks made with Brawl Weapons.

Attack: Use when attacking with Brawl Weapons.

Grapples and Shoves: You may attempt to grapple or shove Beings that are your size or smaller.

  • Grapple: Grappling rules appear on Adventuring.
  • Shove: On a pass, you shove the target 5 feet or knock it Prone. You must have a Brawl or Shield Weapon equipped.

Dexterity

Dexterity determines your Speed and is used for Reflex, Finesse, and Sneak Checks.

Speed (Passive)

How far you can Move on solid ground. Your Speed refreshes at the start of your Turn.

DEX Speed Crawl Travel
2 – 3 25’ 75’ 5 mi
4 – 5 30’ 90’ 6 mi
6 – 7 35’ 105’ 7 mi

Finesse (Skill)

Coordination and concealing your actions. Also used for thievery and when attacking with Finesse Weapons.

Attack: With Finesse Weapons .

Pickpocket: Attempt to steal or plant one unsecured Item of 1 Slot or less.

Lockpick: Check to pick a lock (requires a Lockpick).

Sneak (Skill)

Concealing your location.

Stealth: Attempt to sneak up on a Being you are aware of. You cannot do so if you are carrying a torch or any effect that sheds Light.

Awareness

Awareness is used for Reflex, Detect, Mysticism, and Survival Checks, as well as Ranged Attacks.

Detect (Skill)

Reflexively sensing others and becoming aware of hidden Beings, objects, or threats.

Mysticism (Skill)

Understanding of the supernatural. Used by Druids, Luminaries, and Warlocks to Cast.

Survival (Skill)

Tracking and understanding beasts, plants, natural hazards, and other aspects of the wild.

Ranged (Skill)

Attacking with Ranged Weapons.

Reason

Reason is used for Will, Arcana, Craft, and Medicine Checks.

Arcana (Skill)

Application and knowledge of magic. Used by Magus and Wizards to Cast.

Craft (Skill)

Your ability to appraise and create Items.

Difficulty with Craft determines the highest Value of items you can Craft.

Medicine (Skill)

Education in non-magical healing, treatment, and care.

Presence

Presence is used for Will, Influence, Leadership, and Performance Checks.

Influence (Skill)

Social ability involving coercion, deception, or negotiation. Used by Sorcerers to Cast.

Parley: Attempt to convince an Enemy to end hostilities.

Leadership (Skill)

Social motivation, diplomacy, and the ability to inspire loyalty. Used by Revelators to Cast.

Command: Give orders to those who are willing to follow you.

Performance (Skill)

Cultural talent and the ability to impress, entertain, or inspire awe.

Luck

Luck allows players to tip the scales of Fate and influence events beyond raw skill.

Luck Pool

You have a pool of Luck points equal to your Luck Stat. You regain spent Luck whenever you Rest.

You may spend 1 Luck to do any of the following:

  • Advantage: Grant Favor on a d20 roll.
  • Fluke: Reroll an unresolved die you rolled.
  • Plot Armor: Once per Round, if you would be reduced to 0 HP, you may spend 1 Luck to regain d6 HP.

Misfortune & Chance

When the game needs to determine which Hero is affected by a random effect, the Hero(s) with the lowest current Luck is typically chosen.

Saves

Saves are Checks that represent your instincts and natural defenses against harm. The three Saves are Endure, Reflex, and Will.

Endure

Withstand physical harm such as poisons, extreme heat, restraints, and petrification.

Endure Difficulty = (20 − (MIT × 2))

Reflex

Evade sudden or physical harm, such as explosions, quick traps, slipping grasp, or dragon’s breath.

Reflex Difficulty = (20 − (DEX + AWR))

Will

Resist mental or supernatural harm, such as charms, terrors, psychic influence, or threats to your resolve.

Will Difficulty = (20 − (RSN + PRS))
