Vagabond
Quick Rules
Vagabond is a tabletop roleplaying game built off the foundations of DnD B/X, Pathfinder, Dragonbane, Errant, Knave, Cairn, Into the Odd, Shadowdark, Shadow of the Demon Lord, and other OSR-style games.

Classes

Heroes take on an archetype called a Class. Classes grant Training and Features, and provide a guide for how these Heroes best perform in Combat.

You will gain Levels in your chosen Class, which grant you new and enhanced Features. Features may grant Magic or a Perk, both of which are detailed elsewhere on this website.

Leveling Up

Your advancements as a Hero are signified with a Level in your Class. Each time you gain a Level, you gain all of the following:

  • Your Max HP increases.
  • New and improved Class Features.
  • On Even Levels, increase a single Stat by 1, to a maximum of 7.
  • On Odd Levels after Level 1, you gain one Perk as shown in the Class table.

XP Questionnaire

After each session, ask each of these questions and mark 1 XP for each “yes” you can give.

  • Did you complete a Quest?
  • Did you fail and allow the failure to fully resolve?
  • Did you pass a Hindered Check?
  • Did you make a discovery?
  • Did you loot at least 50g of treasure?

You need 5 × your next level amount of XP to level up.
