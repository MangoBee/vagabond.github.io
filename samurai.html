Quick Rules
Vagabond is a tabletop roleplaying game built off the foundations of DnD B/X, Pathfinder, Dragonbane, Errant, Knave, Cairn, Into the Odd, Shadowdark, Shadow of the Demon Lord, and other OSR-style games.

Samurai

“The way of the samurai is in desperation. Ten men or more cannot kill such a man.”
– Yamamoto Tsunetomo, Hagakure

Samurai embrace the inevitability of death, seeing it as an honor rather than a fear.

Iconic Samurai

  • Isshin Ashina, Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice
  • Shogen Itadori, Sword of the Stranger
  • Kambei Shimada, Seven Samurai

Playing Samurai

The samurai is a medium-high defense striker. In combat, get within effective distance for offense, Focus, and Attack.

  • Action: Attack
  • Move: Flexible
  • Starting Pack: Samurai (yumi) or Samurai (yari)
  • Key Stat: Might or Awareness
  • Complexity: ★☆☆☆☆

Training

  • Weapons: Melee, Ranged
  • Skills: Brawl, Detect, Finesse, and 1 from Influence, Leadership, or Survival

Class Features

Courage

Samurai: 1st Level

You can skip your Move or, as part of an Attack, you can Focus. While Focusing you cannot be Frightened, your Attack Checks and Will Saves are Favored, and your attack damage dice can explode.

Samurai Education

Samurai: 1st Level

You gain the Mounted Combatant Perk and another Perk which you meet the Prerequisites for. You can Swim.

Zanshin

Samurai: 2nd Level

When you hit an Enemy with an Attack, your Saves to Dodge or Block Attacks are Favored until the start of your next Turn.

Clear Mind

Samurai: 4th Level, 8th Level

While Focusing you cannot be Charmed, or Confused. When you become an 8th Level samurai, you cannot be Berserked.

One Stroke

Samurai: 6th Level

When you Crit on an attack, the Enemy is Weak to the damage.

Hesitation is Defeat

Samurai: 10th Level

While Focusing, if you are reduced to 0 HP you can stay up and instead become Vulnerable for Cd4 Rounds. For this duration the roll required for you to Crit on Attack Checks, and Saves to Dodge or Block Attacks is reduced by your Samurai Level. If you take damage while at 0 HP, you die.
