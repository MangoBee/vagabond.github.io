Playing Sanguinist

The sanguinist is a healer, just like the luminary. However, it instead focuses on using Leech to heal themselves and their allies and uses its own HP to Cast Spells.

Variant Archetype (Rule)

Sanguinist: 2nd, 4th, 6th, 10th Levels

You gain the Features for the Luminary at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 10th Levels.

Hematurgy

Sanguinist: 1st Level

You can Cast Spells using Mysticism.

Spells: You learn 4 Spells, two of which must always be Leech and Life. You learn 1 other Spell every 2 Sanguinist Levels hereafter.

Blood Magic: When you Cast, you can reduce your HP to spend Mana on the Cast equal to (3 √ó the reduction), and the highest amount of Mana you can spend on a Cast is equal to (Might + half your Sanguinist Level, round up).

Vitalism

Sanguinist: 1st Level

You gain the Grim Harvest Perk and you can choose to have any healing affecting you instead affect a Near Ally you can sense.

Crimson Power

Sanguinist: 8th Level

While you Focus on Leech, you can Focus on one additional Spell.
